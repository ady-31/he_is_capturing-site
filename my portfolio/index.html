I love photography

"Welcome to 'he_is_capturing,' where I share glimpses of life's moments that spark joy and intrigue, through the lens of my camera. I don't have a strict schedule for photography, rather, I capture images when inspiration strikes or when I stumble upon something fascinating. Whether it's a serene landscape, a soft glow of sunrise, everyday objects bathed in unexpected light, the laughter shared among friends, or simply the play of light on a rainy day, each photo tells a story of discovery and emotion. Join me on this journey of serendipitous discovery and heartfelt appreciation as we uncover the extraordinary in the everyday."
